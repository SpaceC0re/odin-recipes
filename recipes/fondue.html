Fondue Bourguignonne

Convivial French meat dish for six people

Fondue bourguignonne is made of seasoned cubes of raw meat which are to be dipped into boiling oil, cooked for a short while, and eaten with or without sauce.

Ingredients :

  • A fondue pot
  • Beef (1,2kg)
  • Rosemary (One strand)
  • Garlic (One pod)
  • Thyme (One strand)
  • Grapeseed oil (Enough to fill your fondue pot)

Steps :

  1. Cut the beef into small cubes that can easily be picked.
  2. Peel the garlic pod and rub it inside the fondue pot.
  3. Leave the pod inside the pot and add salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the oil inside the pot and start heating it. Add rosemary and thyme.
  5. Use a fondue pick to pick up the beef cubes and dip them inside the boiling oil. Remove them once cooked to your liking.
