Onigiri

Simple Japanese dish made up of a rice ball wrapped in algae

The Onigiri is an easy recipe where we'll fill a ball of rice with meat or fish and wrap it in algae

Ingredients (Tuna and cucumber Onigiri) :

  • 200 grams of Japanese rice
  • 4 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • One can of tuna
  • One piece of cucumber approximately 6cm in length
  • Soy sauce
  • Nori algae

Steps :

  1. Prepare the rice : Soak the rice for 1 hour in cold water. Drain the rice and let it sit for 30 minutes in a strainer.
  2. In a cooking pot, add the rice along with 20cl of water.
  3. Let the rice cook on medium heat, and cover the pot when water starts boiling. Cook for 5 minutes without removing the cover, then lower the heat and let cook for 5 more minutes.
  4. Remove the pot from the stove without removing the cover and let sit for 10 minutes.
  5. Place the rice in a bowl and add the rice vinegar. Let the preparation cool at ambient temperature.
  6. Cut the cucumber in tiny pieces and add the tuna. Mix them with soy sauce.
  7. Humidify your hands in cold water. Take a 50g ball of rice and pack it tight with your hands. Make a small hole in the center and add some of the tuna filling, then close the rice ball around it.
  8. Shape the rice ball into a triangle.
  9. Wrap the rice triangle with algae.
  10. Repeat the process with more rice and more filling.
